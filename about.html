Who Are We?

inGenius X is 501(c)3 nonprofit organization run by high school students in the Northern Virginia area. We seek to empower and educate the next generation of students using novel technology and innovation, providing access to education for those in need. inGenius X publishes apps, hosts events, partners with organizations, and venture funds for educational causes.

In addition to providing state-of-the-art technology to enhance the education of the less fortunate, we also investigate and research new technologies that can be created to provide students with upward mobility. The majority of our research pretains to the creation and implementation of artificial intelligent tutors into the classroom. In doing so, we hyperperzonalize learning for ALL students.
