About Us

A Student Run Nonprofit

inGenius X is 100% nonprofit organization run by high school students in the Northern Virginia area. We seek to empower and educate the next generation of students using novel technology and innovation, providing access to education for those in need. inGenius X publishes apps, hosts events, partners with local organizations, and venture funds for educational causes.
