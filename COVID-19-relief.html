COVID-19 Relief

Partnership with UCA

(United Chinese Americans)

FOOD OF LOVE DRIVE
During the COVID-19 crisis, inGenius X has partnered with and donated $2,500 to the national 501(c)3 nonprofit United Chinese Americans to host the Food of Love Day food drive.

About the Food of Love Day food drive: *From UCA's official statement
UCA has coordinated with Chinese American communities in all 50 states and more than 100 cities to donate food to the people and institutions hit hardest by the new coronavirus. On May 10th, with funds raised through Chinese civic organizations, Chinese restaurants will prepare food and distribute it for free to all patrons at participating locations. On the same day, many of these establishments will also deliver hot meals to homeless shelters, senior centers, hospitals, and soup kitchens. Chinese American communities will also organize food drives on May 9th and 10th to help replenish the fast-dwindling stocks of food banks and food pantries across the country.

Read more about inGenius X's partnership with UCA in this international news feature.

AmeliaX

A free, online virtual learning assistant

To aid in digital learning curriculums during the COVID-19 crisis, inGenius X recently published AmeliaX, a virtual learning chatbot able to autonomously assist students throughout online learning. AmeliaX was designed in the face of distance learning curriculums across the world and in local communitites. It can be used to help with homework, studying, and general learning.
