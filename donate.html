Donate

We're thrilled that you're on board to support our mission!

Due to inGenius X's status as a IRS-certified 501(c)3, we have strict regulations on the donations we receive and how they are reported on annual tax forms. However, we're proud to say we've received donations all the way from national corporations such as Prudential Financial to educational institutions like the University of Delware, to individual crowdfunders.

If you'd like to donate as an individual contributor, please first email us at official.ingeniusx@gmail.com or send a check made out to "inGenius X" to the following address: 1882 Cold Creek Ct, Vienna, VA 22182

If you are a corporation seeking to donate a grant, please first email us at official.ingeniusx@gmail.com or leave a message at +1(703)488-8283.

Thank you!
