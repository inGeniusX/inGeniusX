Apps for Education

Our main intiative consists of our educational app developments. With one published app on the appstore, two pending certification, and three more in progress, inGenius X looks to fuel the rise in the STEM by providing free, high level education to those with a lack in resources. From the basics of math and physics, our apps dive deep into the fields of artificial intelligence and organic chemistry to allow for a truly in-depth experience for those wanting to learn.

Our team spends hours every week to ensure the quality and accuracy of every single app. We guarentee the satisfaction of our students with live customer support built into every one of our downloads.

HackGenius 2020

As a student of a school with a majority of its student body being economically deprived, Andrew has seen the effects of poverty on one's education. Without the necessary recourses, students simply cannot reach the heights of society. As a hands-on initiative of inGenius X, HackGenius is a free of all costs hackathon at the University of North America. Not only will students explore new concepts and enrich themselves in the spirit of competition, students will have the chance to meet with mentors and keynote speakers to create life long mentorships. At inGenius X, we put the resources and opportunities in the hands of everyone so that we can get one step closer to solving global inequalities.

Local Partnerships & Research

Additionally, inGenius X looks to innovate education by conducting state-of-the-art research. With Rayan's artificial intelligence background and Andrew's app development experience they work avidly to make novel developments in the intersection of education and technology.

Currently, inGenius X is working on inGeniusLearn, a mobile platform for hyperperzonalized education with the introduction of Amelia, a fully autonomous intelligent tutor. Amelia's novel machine learning capabilities allow her to virtually replace the teacher in the classroom and orient content directly towards each individual student. Additionally, she can make decisive verbal responses to each user and noninvasively detect stress signals, giving her unprecedented capability.
