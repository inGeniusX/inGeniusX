Project Edutivity - Apps for Education

Our first intiative consists of our educational app developments. With two published apps on the appstore, two pending certification, and two more in progress, inGenius X looks to fuel the rise in the STEM by providing free, high level education to those with a lack in resources. From the basics of math and physics, our apps dive deep into the fields of artificial intelligence and organic chemistry to allow for a truly in-depth experience for those wanting to learn.

Our lead mobile app developer, Andrew Tran, spends hours every week with the team to ensure the quality and accuracy of every single app. We guarentee the satisfaction of our students with live customer support built into every one of our downloads.

HackGenius 2020 (DELAYED DUE TO COVID)

HackGenius is a free, 24-hour computer science event meant for underpriviliged students seeking STEM opportunity. With all the resources available in the Northern Virginia region, inGenius X seeks to equalize STEM opportunity for those in underrepresented and underprivileged areas. As a hands-on initiative of inGenius X, HackGenius is a free of all costs hackathon at the University of North America. Not only will students explore new concepts and enrich themselves in the spirit of competition, students will have the chance to meet with mentors and keynote speakers to create life long mentorships. At inGenius X, we put the resources and opportunities in the hands of everyone so that we can get one step closer to solving global inequalities.

Novel Research

inGenius X looks to innovate education by conducting state-of-the-art research. With Rayan's artificial intelligence background and Andrew's app development experience they work avidly to make novel developments in the intersection of education and technology.

inGeniusLearn

inGenius X has recently published inGeniusLearn, a digital software to hyperperzonalize education. inGeniusLearn employs deep neural networks to immediately recognize student confusion through facial cues, and provides immediate feedback by parsing lectures sections and matching information to web databases. inGeniusLearn gradually builds a personal student profile to understand user learning patterns, areas of strength and weakness, etc. inGeniusLearn is founded on an educational psychology basis, with the student-tutor relationship at the core of its function. inGeniusLearn was named the first place winner of the Regional Diamond Challenge to receive $1,500 in seed funding. inGeniusLearn is currently representing the region at the global finals round. UPDATE: inGeniusLearn has been awarded the National 1st Place and International Runner-up at the Global Diamond Challenge. inGenius X has been awarded an additional $7,000 in seed funding.
