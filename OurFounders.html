Our Founders

Andrew Tran

CEO & Co-Founder || tranandrew30@gmail.com

Andrew, a student at Justice High School, is a web developer, mobile app developer, and social entrepreneur. His interest for computer science originated as a fourth grader on Khan Academy. Andrew started coding not because he wanted to learn all of java or python, but because he wanted to make cool games and websites for his friends his family. His interest in coding later developed into a passion that carried throughout middle and high school. Andrew is now the creator of three iOS & Android apps, the founder of his school’s App Development Club, and an avid programmer. With inGeniusX, he hopes to share his experience and democratize STEM education to anyone, regardless of their socieconomic conditions.

Rayan Yu

President & Co-Founder || rayan.yu@gmail.com

Rayan Yu, a junior at James Madison High School, is an artificial intelligence researcher, investment enthusiast, and social entrepreneur. Since his childhood, Rayan has been interested in computer science, specifically, the field of AI and its application throughout the world. Currently, he is the founder of his school’s machine learning club (madAI, sponsored by Northrop Grumman), an author of deep learning research, and an avid participant in computing competitions. Aside from computer science, Rayan loves playing basketball, public speaking, watching financial markets, and reading about philosophy. With inGeniusX, he seeks to introduce computer science and AI to all students; to find the hidden talents and interests of those that could be just around the corner.
