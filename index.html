About inGenius X

Challenge the status quo of education.

inGenius X is a 501(c)3 nonprofit organization founded by high school students in the Northern Virginia area.

We aim to make modern education accessible and personal by publishing apps to provide learning resources and developing software platforms to enhance digital learning experiences.

Besides designing software and apps, inGenius X hosts events, partners with international organizations, and fundraises for educational causes. We work to make learning easy and personal for any student anywhere.
