Venture Funding

inGenius X devotes all profits to the funding of international and domestic ventures for the improvement of education. From building schools to solarizing facilities, inGenius X is always on the lookout for a location of need that can be helped through their efforts.

Gallery

inGeniusX cares not just about giving back, but also the method in how. Our efforts prioritize looking for new, innovative ideas that can assist in the worldwide lack of education. At inGenius X, we cultivate state-of-the-art solutions to educate those in need.

About us

inGenius X is a 100% nonprofit, student-run organization founded for a single goal: to foster the minds of those lacking suitable resources. By integrating modern technology with timeless education, inGenius X creates and publishes free-of-cost mobile apps for learning STEM. In addition, inGenius X funds ventures around the globe for the cause of education, persistently seeking ways to further the knowledge of those in need.
